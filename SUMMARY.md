## Curriculum Overview

### Module 1: Organizational Behavior & HR (10 lessons)
1. Introduction to Organizational Behavior
2. Motivation and Performance
3. Leadership and Management
4. Team Dynamics and Collaboration
5. Organizational Culture and Change
6. Recruitment and Selection
7. Performance Management
8. Compensation and Benefits
9. Employee Development and Training
10. Employee Relations and Workplace Ethics

### Module 2: Marketing Management (10 lessons)
Complete framework covering marketing fundamentals, consumer behavior, segmentation, branding, product development, pricing, distribution, communications, digital marketing, and analytics.

### Module 3: Financial Management (10 lessons)
Corporate finance, financial analysis, time value of money, capital budgeting, cost of capital, capital structure, dividends, working capital, risk analysis, and financial planning.

### Module 4: Operations & Supply Chain (10 lessons)
Operations management, process design, capacity planning, quality systems, lean operations, supply chain strategy, inventory, logistics, supplier relationships, and analytics.

### Module 5: Strategic Management (10 lessons)
Strategy fundamentals, environmental analysis, industry analysis, internal analysis, SWOT, competitive strategies, corporate strategy, international strategy, implementation, and control.

### Module 6: Business Communication (10 lessons)
Communication fundamentals, written communication, email etiquette, report writing, presentations, interpersonal skills, negotiations, cross-cultural communication, crisis communication, and digital tools.

### Module 7: Business Analytics (10 lessons)
Analytics introduction, descriptive analytics, data visualization, predictive analytics, prescriptive analytics, statistical analysis, A/B testing, customer analytics, operations analytics, and BI tools.

### Module 8: Entrepreneurship & Innovation (10 lessons)
Entrepreneurship fundamentals, opportunity recognition, business models, startup financing, lean startup, product-market fit, scaling, innovation management, corporate entrepreneurship, and leadership.

### Module 9: Ethics & Governance (10 lessons)
Business ethics, ethical frameworks, CSR, stakeholder theory, governance principles, board roles, regulatory compliance, sustainability, ethical leadership, and global ethics.

### Module 10: Global Business & Leadership (10 lessons)
Global business introduction, international trade, market entry, cross-cultural management, global strategy, international operations, executive leadership, strategic decisions, change leadership, and future of leadership.
